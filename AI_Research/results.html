The AI model trained with the Facebook Prophet API successfully forecast crises in social media And we can also use social media content to predict future crises.
There is a recurring pattern in which each year there is a major crisis in social media Therefore, this model is important for monitoring social media content because it can serve as an early warning system.
This provides valuable information for crisis management and implementing strategies.

• By analyzing social media content and identifying patterns, the model can serve as an early warning system. This allows organizations to anticipate and prepare for potential crises before they fully develop.

• The model is adept at recognizing recurring patterns, such as the annual occurrence of major social media crises. This predictive capability is crucial for timely interventions.

• Leveraging historical and real-time data, the model provides data-driven insights that enhance the accuracy of forecasts. This leads to more informed decision-making.

• With early detection of crises, organizations can implement proactive crisis management strategies. This reduces the impact of crises and helps maintain stability and reputation.

• By predicting crises in advance, organizations can allocate resources more effectively, ensuring that the right measures are in place when needed.

• Continuous monitoring and analysis of social media content improve overall preparedness, allowing organizations to respond swiftly and effectively to emerging issues.

• The model's forecasts support long-term strategic planning by identifying potential risks and trends, enabling better preparation for future challenges.
