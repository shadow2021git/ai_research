Background

This research study is conducted for the final year research model of Bachelors of Information Communication Technology. This research study is carried out to forecast crisis events on social media using machine learning model and time series data.
Social media enables real times sharing of multimedia content which allows individuals and organizations to quickly disseminate updated information during a crisis And also, social media allows community engagement to share experience Individuals can enhance the overall situational awareness during crisis.
However social media offers disadvantages like rapid spread of toxic, harmful information As per the studies, 1 8 billion photos are posted and shared over various social media platforms daily.
Our research is focused on develop an AI tool which can forecast crisis events to identify the which kind of contents actually published on social media platforms.
So, we are going to analyze the contents spread during crisis like Israel P alestine war, Sri Lankan protests 2022 Then develop an AI model to forecast the violence, terrorists etc

Research problem

Research problem 1 - How can we develop AI tools to handle large volumes of data on social media during crisis? To handling large volumes of data on social media during crises, we can use preprocessing techniques, Natural language processing, machine learning models, visualization tools.

Research problem 2 - What methodologies and algorithms are suitable to identify misinformation? Text classifications, sentiment analysis, social network analyzing, supervised and unsupervised learning methods and algorithms can be used to identify misinformation.

Research problem 3 - How can AI tools be developed to analyze different types of multimedia content? Deep learning frameworks, feature extraction, computer vision, audio processing, time series analysis tools can be developed to analyze different types of multimedia content.

Research problem 4 - How AI tools be developed to adapt with changing crisis? Real-time data analyzing, anomaly detection, dynamic models, feedback loops can be integrating to adapt with crisis.

Main objective

Our main objective is to develop AI tool capable of forecasting crisis events. We gather historical and real-time data from various sources. Then identified relevant features indicative of crisis events such as trends, anomalies. We developed machine learning model for time series analyzes to predict future crisis events based on identified features. Then transform the model to learn from new data and improve the accurate forecasting. Finally developed dashboard to visualize results.

Specific objectives

• Understand human behavior on social media - Get an idea about why humans behave on certain way in specific times.

• Analyze the flow of crisis’ step begin to end - Understand social media crisis from its initial stage to end of it can be useful for decision makers to face ongoing challenges and future challenges.

• Contribute to Academic and Industry Knowledge enhancement – Gain knowledge important for academic works and industry works.
